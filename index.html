Collaboration FAQs

How can I collaborate with WeLinQ?

Established experts can make their card collections. Collaborated content will feature in "Expert Collaboration" in WeLinQ app.

Card collections can be based on different themes, stage in relationship etc. The idea will be to create questions that will encourage users to understand their partner better.

What's in it for me?

  • WeLinQ can help you reach out to more individuals, in particular couples who are looking to improve their relationship.

  • Gift to your followers.

  • Appear on WeLinQ's expert section.

  • Engage with WeLinQ users.

  • [Future feature] Verified profile on WeLinQ.

    • What is WeLinQ's target audience?

    WeLinQ's audience is mostly who are in relationship, dating, started going out with their partners.

    What other ways I can collaborate?

    On the "We" tab of app, you will find community that seeks answers to different life situations. You can answer questions which you think you are well suited for.

    Will I be credited?

    Ofcourse. Card collections will have your name, profile link on the collection description.

    Can I link my Instagram?

    You can have your Instagram username in your WeLinQ profile description.

    Where will my content appear?

    Collaborated content will feature in "Experts Collaboration" in WeLinQ app.
